Pricing & Process
Power BI dashboards start from ₹15,000 for a standard project. The final price depends on the number of report pages, data sources, complexity of DAX calculations, and whether the data needs ETL pipeline setup. I provide a fixed quote after a free 30-minute discovery call — no hidden costs, no surprises. See full pricing details here.
A standard Power BI dashboard (1 data source, up to 5 pages) is typically delivered in 1–2 weeks. A Full BI Solution with SQL modeling, ETL pipeline, and cloud deployment takes 3–6 weeks. Python automation projects take 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. Timelines are confirmed in writing before work begins.
Under ₹10,000: full upfront. Larger projects: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Simple invoice sent before each payment. Accepted methods: UPI, bank transfer (India), PayPal, and Wise (international). No payment is required before the free discovery call.
Typically I start within 2–3 business days of onboarding and payment. For urgent requirements, same-week starts are possible — just mention it when booking or message me directly on WhatsApp.
Technical Questions
Power BI natively connects to 100+ sources. I regularly work with: Excel, Google Sheets, CSV (spreadsheets); MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server, Snowflake (databases); REST APIs, Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, SAP, SharePoint (platforms); and Azure SQL, AWS RDS (cloud). If your source isn't listed, we'll discuss it on the discovery call.
For personal use or sharing via a public link, Power BI Free is enough. To share reports securely within your organization (behind login, with role-level security), Power BI Pro is required (~₹700/user/month or included in Microsoft 365 E3/E5). I'll recommend the right licensing during the discovery call based on your team size and sharing needs.
