Who Is This For?

The Full BI Solution is for teams that have outgrown spreadsheets and need a proper, scalable data infrastructure — not just a pretty dashboard on top of messy data.

  • Teams with data scattered across 3+ different sources (CRM, ERP, APIs, Excel)
  • Businesses that need automated pipelines, not manual exports
  • Founders preparing for investor reporting or board reviews
  • Organizations moving to cloud-hosted BI for the first time
  • Teams that previously tried cheap solutions and got burned

What's Included (5 Deliverables)

  • SQL data model + star schema design — Clean, normalized database structure optimized for reporting. Covers fact tables, dimension tables, and KPI logic.
  • Python ETL pipeline with scheduled refresh — Extracts data from your sources, transforms it, and loads it into the warehouse automatically. Handles errors, logs, and incremental updates.
  • Azure cloud deployment & access control — Pipeline and database hosted on Azure. Role-based access so the right people see the right data. Gateway setup for scheduled Power BI refresh.
  • Full Power BI dashboard suite (up to 10 pages) — Executive overview, department drilldowns, trend analysis, and custom KPI views. Mobile-ready and branded.
  • 90 days of support, training & documentation — Full handover package: written docs, walkthrough recordings, and 90 days of direct support. I'll train your team to use and maintain it.
