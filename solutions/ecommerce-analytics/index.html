Power BI E-commerce Dashboard — Revenue, Orders & Customer Insights in One View

D2C brands and online retailers make decisions on yesterday's Shopify export and a gut feeling. I'll build a live Power BI dashboard that shows your revenue, top products, AOV, customer LTV, and return rate — updated automatically, no manual exports needed.

1–2 Week Delivery 25+ Projects Delivered Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon

Who Is This For?

  • D2C brand founders who need daily revenue and order visibility without logging into Shopify 10 times
  • E-commerce managers tracking product performance, returns, and customer cohorts
  • Multi-channel sellers combining Shopify, Amazon, and WooCommerce data into one view
  • Retail businesses tracking in-store vs online sales alongside inventory levels

What's Tracked in an E-commerce Dashboard

  • Revenue & Orders — Daily/weekly/monthly sales, order volume, and GMV trends
  • Average Order Value (AOV) — Trend over time, by channel and product category
  • Top Products — Best sellers by revenue, volume, and margin
  • Customer LTV & Repeat Rate — New vs returning customers, cohort analysis
  • Return Rate — Returns by product, SKU, and reason code
  • Channel Performance — Revenue split by Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce, wholesale
  • Marketing Attribution — Revenue by UTM source, campaign, and ad spend (if GA4 data available)
