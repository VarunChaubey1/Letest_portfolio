Executive KPI Dashboard — The Full Business Picture for Leadership Teams

Leadership shouldn't need to chase numbers from 4 different team leads before every board meeting. I'll build a single Power BI dashboard that pulls from all your data sources and gives every executive a live, accurate view of the business — with no manual work.

1–2 Week Delivery 25+ Projects Delivered 30-Day Support Row-Level Security

Who Is This For?

CEOs, COOs, and leadership teams who need a reliable single source of truth instead of juggling 5 department reports every Monday morning.

  • CEOs who need a daily pulse on revenue, growth, and operational health
  • Leadership teams preparing weekly reviews or board presentations
  • Founders who want one dashboard that replaces weekly status emails from each team
  • Companies with data spread across CRM, finance, ops, and HR systems

What Goes Into an Executive KPI Dashboard

  • Revenue & Growth KPIs — MRR, ARR, YoY growth, revenue vs target
  • Customer Metrics — Acquisition, churn, NPS, LTV, CAC
  • Financial Health — Gross margin, burn rate, cash position, EBITDA
  • Sales Pipeline — Pipeline value, conversion rates, close velocity
  • Operational KPIs — Delivery timelines, SLA adherence, team utilization
  • Department Scorecards — Each team's key metrics in one view with drill-through
  • Board-Ready Visuals — Clean, branded design that can be shared directly in meetings

Multi-Source Data Integration

Executive dashboards need data from multiple systems. I build the data model that joins them cleanly:

Sales & CRM

Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho — pipeline, revenue, and customer data.

Finance

QuickBooks, Xero, SAP — P&L, cash flow, budget vs actuals.

Operations

Project tools, support systems, HR data — delivery and team metrics.

Custom SQL

Any PostgreSQL, MySQL, or Snowflake database — direct connection.
