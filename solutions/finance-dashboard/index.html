Power BI Finance Dashboard — P&L, Cash Flow & Budget vs Actuals in One View

Finance teams spend hours rebuilding the same P&L report in Excel every month. I'll build a live Power BI finance dashboard that pulls from your accounting system or SQL database and updates automatically — so you can spend time on analysis, not data entry.

1–2 Week Delivery 25+ Projects Delivered 30-Day Support NDA Available

Who Is This For?

CFOs, finance managers, and founders who need accurate financial visibility without spending 6 hours rebuilding the same Excel model every month-end.

  • Finance heads who need real-time P&L, cash position, and budget vs actuals
  • CFOs preparing board reports who want data that updates itself
  • Founders tracking burn rate, MRR, and profitability across departments
  • Finance teams using QuickBooks, Xero, Tally, SAP, or custom ERP systems

What's Tracked in a Finance Dashboard

  • P&L Statement — Revenue, COGS, gross margin, operating expenses, EBITDA
  • Budget vs Actuals — Variance analysis by department and cost center
  • Cash Flow Tracker — Operating, investing, and financing activities by period
  • Expense Breakdown — Spend by category, vendor, and team with drill-through
  • Revenue by Stream — MRR, ARR, one-time vs recurring, by product or region
  • Month-over-Month Trends — Revenue growth, margin trends, working capital changes
  • Accounts Receivable / Payable — Aging buckets, overdue alerts, collection velocity

Data Sources I Connect

Accounting Software

QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Tally — via export or API.

ERP Systems

SAP, Oracle, Dynamics — via SQL connection or structured export.

Excel / Sheets

Existing financial models migrated into a proper data structure.

Cloud Databases

Azure SQL, PostgreSQL, Snowflake — direct connection with scheduled refresh.
