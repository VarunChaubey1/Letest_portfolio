Power BI Sales Dashboard — Pipeline, Revenue & Rep Performance in One View

Stop rebuilding your sales report every Monday. I'll build a live Power BI dashboard that shows your pipeline health, revenue vs target, top performers, and conversion rates — updated automatically from your CRM or data source.

1–2 Week Delivery 25+ Projects Delivered 30-Day Support CRM + SQL + Sheets

Who Is This For?

Sales managers, founders, and revenue leaders who are tired of chasing numbers from their CRM every week to paste into a PowerPoint slide.

  • Sales managers who need to see rep performance, pipeline, and close rates in real time
  • Founders who want to track MRR, deal velocity, and revenue vs target without opening Salesforce
  • RevOps teams building a single source of truth for sales data
  • Teams using HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho CRM, or a custom SQL database

What's Tracked in a Sales Dashboard

  • Revenue vs Target — MTD, QTD, YTD actuals compared to quota
  • Pipeline by Stage — deals by funnel stage with conversion rates
  • Rep Leaderboard — individual performance by volume, value, and close rate
  • Deal Velocity — average days from first contact to close
  • Win / Loss Analysis — where deals are being lost and why
  • Forecast vs Actual — weighted pipeline forecast compared to target
  • Product / SKU breakdown — revenue by product line or region
  • Trend analysis — MoM revenue growth, churn, and seasonal patterns

Data Sources I Connect

CRM Systems

Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive — via API connector or export.

SQL Databases

PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server, Snowflake — direct connection or ETL pipeline.

Spreadsheets

Google Sheets, Excel — cleaned and modelled into a proper data structure.

REST APIs

Custom APIs, Stripe, QuickBooks — via Python ETL pipeline if no native connector exists.
